SEO Expertise

I specialize in improving website visibility and driving organic traffic. Below are some of the SEO strategies I have used to enhance website performance.

1. On-Page SEO Optimization

Project: Optimized the content and meta tags of a blog to improve keyword targeting.

  • Focused on high-value keywords for blog posts.
  • Optimized title tags, meta descriptions, and headings.
  • Implemented alt text for all images for better accessibility and ranking.

Result: Achieved a 25% increase in search engine traffic within 3 months.

2. Link Building Campaign

Project: Built high-quality backlinks for a business website to improve domain authority.

  • Identified relevant industry websites for guest posts and collaborations.
  • Outreached to influencers and bloggers for backlinks.
  • Created shareable content to naturally attract backlinks.

Result: Improved domain authority by 15 points and saw a 20% increase in organic traffic.

3. Local SEO Optimization

Project: Optimized a local business website to improve visibility in local search results.

  • Created and optimized a Google My Business profile.
  • Ensured NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone Number) across all directories.
  • Added local keywords to content and meta tags to target the local audience.

Result: Increased local search visibility and foot traffic to the business by 30%.
